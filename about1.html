JAC Motors in Nepal

EARLY 2010

and after thorough research on the best brand that combines value for money, Chidiac Motors decided to acquire the rights to represent JAC, the leading Chinese automotive brand, in Lebanon. Hence, becoming its sole agent in the Lebanese market.

JAC motors was officially launched in the Lebanese market taking its headquarter in Beirut where most of the competitors’ headquarters are located. By end of 2012, Chidiac Motors successfully established key points of sale for JAC, and initiated its 4S showroom project in one of the most strategic locations in the Lebanese automotive industry. JAC’s 4S project was accomplished and launched in early 2014

Why JAC?

Given its vast experience in the automotive industry, Chidiac Motors not only knew the market inside out, it also had the will & determination to bring the best brand that serves its needs & meets the demand criteria.
  • Italian Design: All the JAC cars are designed in Italy's Turin, which is unurguably the center of excellence & a source of reference in design
  • Japanese Technology: JAC cars are powered by Japanese technology to give an outstanding performance & greater driving pleasure
  • Bosch Safety Standards: What good is design & technology if there's no safety? Hence, all the JAC cars are equipped by Bosch safety standards. They're German, need we say more?
  • Fuel Efficiency: Given the traffic, the geography of our country, and the icreasing fuel prices, Chidiac Motors made sure to bring a brand that is fuel efficient & pocket friendly. JAC Motors meets both criteria
  • Environment Friendly: JAC Motors meets the Euro IV requirements & the brand itself is careful to create long term sustainable vehicles that serve both human & the environment
  • Low Spare Parts Prices: JAC has the most competitive spare parts prices in the Lebanese market & a dedicated after sales service center to ensure customer satisfaction with the brand
